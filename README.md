## 🇺🇸 English Version

### 🚀 What is This Project?

**Ultimate Age Calculator** is a free, open-source web application meticulously designed to give you far more than just your age — it's a **complete journey** through your life!

> *"Not just an age calculator — it's a mirror to your entire existence"*

---

### ✨ Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| 🎂 **Exact Age** | Years + Months + Days with pinpoint accuracy |
| ⏱️ **Live Counter** | Hours, minutes & seconds updating in real-time |
| ♑ **Western Zodiac** | Your star sign with its symbol |
| 🐉 **Chinese Zodiac** | Your animal in the Chinese calendar |
| 💎 **Birthstone** | The precious gem of your birth month |
| 🌸 **Birth Flower** | The symbolic flower of your birth month |
| 🌕 **Moon Phase** | The shape of the moon on your birthday |
| 🕌 **Hijri Age** | Your age in the Islamic calendar |
| 📅 **Birth Day** | The day of the week you were born |
| 🎊 **Countdown** | Time remaining until your next birthday |
| 💓 **Body Stats** | Your heartbeats, breaths & sleep hours |
| 🏆 **Life Milestones** | Upcoming major milestones in your life |
| 🎉 **Confetti** | A special surprise on your birthday! |
| 📸 **Share** | Save your results as an image |
| 🎨 **3 Themes** | Default 🌌 · Royal Gold 👑 · Matrix 💻 |
